Hostel life at Poornima University offers a wide range of unique pleasures that are different from the comforts of home.

About Our Hostel

The History Of Our Hostel

The hostel at Poornima University is a legendary rite of passage. With creaky beds, silent Wi-Fi, surprise water outages, and a gourmet menu of culinary mysteries, it promises character-building through sheer necessity. Students come with dreams and leave as warriors of survival, armed with tales to last a lifetime.

Read More

Great Location

Nestled strategically at the far end of the campus, the hostel boasts the perfect location for cardio enthusiasts who love a daily hike. Conveniently placed just out of reach for when you're late to class, it ensures that every dash is a mini-marathon.

Included Meals

The meals at the hostel are a masterclass in suspense, where every dish is a plot twist. From the mysterious "paneer surprise" to the eternally soupy dal, each bite is an adventurous leap of faith. It's a dining experience that teaches true appreciation—for the snack stash hidden in your room.

Fitness Room

The hostel gym at Poornima University is where motivation goes to test its patience. With equipment that could double as relics from a museum, it offers a unique workout experience—lifting weights that may or may not squeak back at you. It’s the perfect place to master bodyweight exercises while waiting for the one treadmill to become available.
